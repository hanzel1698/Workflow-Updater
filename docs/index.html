Privacy Policy

RDO KKD Workflow Updater (package: in.rdokk.workflowupdater)
Last updated: 3 July 2026

This app is an internal workflow viewer for the RDO KKD design office. It displays work data from a shared Google Sheet and lets you export PDF reports. This policy explains what the app does with information on your device.

Information we collect

The app does not collect, sell, or transmit personal data to the developer.

  • Google Sheet data — When you use the app, it downloads workflow rows from a Google Apps Script endpoint connected to the office spreadsheet. That data is shown on screen and may be included in PDF exports you create.
  • On-device preferences — Your selected engineer profile, default profile choice, and release-notes “seen” state are stored locally on your phone using Android SharedPreferences. This data stays on your device.
  • PDF export — When you export a report, the name you enter is used only in the generated PDF title and file name on your device. It is not sent to the developer.

Information we do not collect

  • No account sign-in inside the app
  • No analytics or advertising SDKs
  • No crash reporting services operated by the developer
  • No contact list, location, camera, microphone, or SMS access

Network use

The app requires Internet access to load live workflow data from Google’s servers (Apps Script / Google Sheets). If the network is unavailable, the app may show previously loaded or sample data where implemented.

Third parties

Data requests go to Google (Apps Script / Sheets) as configured for the RDO KKD workflow sheet. Google’s own privacy policies apply to how Google processes that traffic. The developer does not operate a separate backend server for this app.

Children

This app is intended for professional use by RDO KKD staff and is not directed at children under 13.

Data retention and deletion

Local preferences remain until you clear app data or uninstall the app. To remove on-device data: Android Settings → Apps → RDO KKD Workflow Updater → Storage → Clear data, or uninstall the app.

Changes

We may update this policy when the app changes. The “Last updated” date above will be revised accordingly.

Contact
