Workflow Updater

Sign in with your authorized Google account to open the dashboard.

Access is limited to approved email addresses configured in config.js.

Firebase setup required
  1. Create a project in the Firebase Console.
  2. Add a Web app and copy the Firebase config into windows/config.js under AUTH.FIREBASE.
  3. Enable Google as a sign-in provider in Authentication → Sign-in method.
  4. Add your app origin (for example localhost and your deployed domain) under Authentication → Settings → Authorized domains.
  5. List allowed users in AUTH.ALLOWED_EMAILS in config.js.
